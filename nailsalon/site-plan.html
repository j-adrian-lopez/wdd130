About Us

Meet our technician!
If we were to describe Laura in one word, we would be forced to say "creative". An outstanding artist, she is certified in nail beauty and care, and has more than four years of experience in the field. She is a passionate advocate of wellness and good looks, and a talented nail designer. She got certified in gel nails with the Maura Nail Design team, and the DermoBeauty multi-salon company. She has also earned a certification in the use of nail lathes, becoming an expert when it comes to sanding, filing, and removing old gel polish with the aid of tools.
